Simple Foodie API

Raw text, no intrusive ads, vegetarian recipes

There are a few ways you can use this API...

You can query the entire database; a specific recipe by ID; perform a search by title, ingredients, directions and the text you want to search by; or view a plaintext version of that recipe by ID.


    /recipes-api/ /recipes-api/int:recipe_id /recipes-api/str:field/str:query /int:recipe_id
